Design Principles Document

Fitt's Law - Walmart

Walmart

Walmart does a great job of displaying Fitt's Law. This homepage is easy and fast to interact with. Each of the dipslays are large, and the whole of it is a clickable button.

PARC: Proximity - Airbnb

Airbnb

The airbnb homepage is neat by keeping "like" content in the same proximity. Available listing are one after another, categories are listed above with a filter button right next to them, and above that are 3 simple inputs to narrow your search.

PARC: Contrast - Apple

Apple

Just a glance and you will see contrast to a max. Apple's use of high contrast, black an white, shows distinct seperation between sections and boldly showcases their product as its brightness and shine contrast the background.
