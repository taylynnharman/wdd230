Overview

Purpose

Our mission includes advocating for local business interests, providing networking opportunities, promoting local enterprises, and contributing to economic development. We facilitate connections among businesses, offer educational resources, support community initiatives, and represent our members' interests to local government.

Audience

Entrepreneurs, start-ups, new & potential businesses, existing business, investors

Scenarios

  • Where can I find details about upcoming workshops and training sessions offered by the chamber?
  • How can I get involved in community service or volunteering opportunities through the chamber?
  • Is there a section on the website where I can access resources and guides for starting a new business in Highland?

This page contains information about the benefit of joining the chamber and a membership application form. There are three membership level options of non-profit, silver, and gold.
